Smart Work Learning

Our Mission

To Revolutionize the Way Students Learn by Filling the Missing Gap

There is very little time in a day, yet you have so much to learn. Give us an hour, and you will see the difference. Smart Work Learning (SWL) uses a Peer-to-Peer tutoring method. Our unique three-pronged approach is used to achieve meaningful student academic success.

Tutoring will take place online, with a minimum of two sessions per week.

Smart Work Learning (SWL) will communicate regularly with families and tutors to track student's academic progress and net educational gains. In general, tutoring sessions will not be scheduled at times when the student would otherwise be participating in same activities in the regular classroom.

Our tutors are meticulously selected. SWL provides all students with the personal attention they look for in their academic experience. Smart Work Learning takes great pride in the various services and programs it provides to all students. They will achieve their academic goals.

Our program is designed with YOU in mind. Smart Work Learning (SWL) prides itself in delivering the best academic Peer-to-Peer experience by meeting YOU where you are in your studies. SWL also educate parents by giving positive and negative feedback during the tutoring process.

What sets us apart?

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Because we research what works and what doesn't work with today's students, we are better able to hone in on what matters most. Students are not able to get the full benefit of the traditional teaching session because they are:

  1. Afraid to ask questions because they are afraid of being labeled
  2. Afraid of disrupting the teacher
  3. Afraid of being stereotyped

We focus on correcting those things by allowing all our kids to participate in our initial interview.

The first thing we do...

During the students' interview process

  1. We quickly identify student weaknesses and strengths
  2. We EVALUATE the student’s abilities to identify their current levels
  3. We IMPLEMENT a strategically created method to improve their abilities academically
  4. And finally, we DEVELOP work with our tutors to develop short and long-term measurable academic goals and placing them with the right tutor

Benefits

  • Early preparation for virtual work etc.
  • Flexibility in scheduling to include weekends
  • One to one learning leads to better learning
  • Better way to monitor kids' action in learning
  • Opportunity to meet with tutors from anywhere
  • Students felt more in control of their learning process by spending more time on concepts that they need more help in and less in others
